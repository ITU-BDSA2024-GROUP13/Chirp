Naval Fate.

Usage:
    $ ship new <name>...
    $ ship <name> move <x> <y> [--speed=<kn>]
    $ ship shoot <x> <y>
    $ mine (set|remove) <x> <y> [--moored | --drifting]
    $ (-h | --help)
    $ --version

Options:
    -h --help     Show this screen.
    --version     Show version.
    --speed=<kn>  Speed in knots [default: 10].
    --moored      Moored (anchored) mine.
    --drifting    Drifting mine.
